Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap simply translates to "mixed rice with meat and assorted vegetables". You can make endless variations to this dish depending on your preference and dietary requirements.

Ingredients

  • 100g beef mince
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 tsp minced garlic
  • 250g seasoned spinach
  • 350g seasoned bean sprouts
  • 100g shiitake mushroom
  • 120g carrots
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 3-4 serving portions of steamed rice
  • 3-4 eggs
  • Toasted seasoned seaweed
  • 2 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 Tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp vinegar

Stesp

  1. Mix the beef mince with the soy sauce, 1 Tbsp of sesame oil, the brown sugar and 1/4 tsp minced garlic. Marinate the meat for 30 minutes while you are working on the other ingredients. Add some cooking oil into a wok and cook the meat on medium to high heat for 3-5 minutes.
  2. In a bowl mix the gochujang, the rest of the sesame oil, the sugar, the water, the roasted sesame seeds, the vinegar and the rest of the minced garlic.
  3. Cook the spinach and bean sprouts.
  4. Rinse, peel and julienne the carrots. Add some cooking oil and half of the sea salt in a wok and cook on a medium to high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Clean/rinse the shiitake mushrooms and thinly slice them. Add some cooking oil and the rest of the salt in a wok and cook them on medium to high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Fry the eggs
  7. Put the rice into a bowl and add the meat, assorted vegetables, seasoned seaweed, bibimbap sauce and the egg on top of the rice.
  8. Mix all the ingredients together and enjoy.
